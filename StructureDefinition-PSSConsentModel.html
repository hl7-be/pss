Logical Model: PSS Consent

Official URL: https://www.ehealth.fgov.be/standards/fhir/pss/StructureDefinition/PSSConsentModel Version: 1.0.0

Other Identifiers: OID:2.16.840.1.113883.2.51.22.2.42.17
